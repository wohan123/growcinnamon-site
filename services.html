What We Do

Our Service Streams

Four distinct services — from a single planting visit to a full multi-year estate management contract. Choose what fits your land and goals.

Service 01

Land Preparation & Development

Whether it's a jungle plot, an abandoned paddy field, or sloped terrain, we prepare it completely for commercial cinnamon cultivation. We protect your topsoil, control water runoff, and lay the physical foundation for a long-term producing estate.

Zero-Erosion Land Clearing

Mechanical brush clearing — no burning. Biomass is shredded back into the soil to preserve organic matter.

Contour Drain Engineering

Physical contour trenches slow water velocity, turning destructive runoff into passive irrigation for the cinnamon root zone.

Planting Position Marking & Hole Digging

Optimal row spacing (4×3 ft flat / 4×2 ft sloped) marked and 1×1×1 ft holes dug — ready for planting day.

Soil pH Testing & Basal Fertilizer

Soil samples from at least 7 points per acre. Dolomite applied to reach the optimal pH 5.5–6.5. Eppawala Rock Phosphate applied per hole.

Ideal for: Landowners with bare, jungle, or unused land who want a complete turnkey preparation before planting.

Service 03

Ongoing Field Maintenance

Cinnamon cultivation requires consistent, expert management between planting and harvest. Our maintenance contracts ensure your estate stays disease-free, well-nourished, and growing optimally — so you don't have to manage it yourself.

Precision Fertilizer Applications

Targeted N:P:K 23:7:15 applications (900 kg/hectare/year) split into scheduled doses. Integrated with organic compost from cinnamon leaf and scraping waste.

Weed Management

Periodic slashing of above-ground weed growth using grass-cutting machinery. Manual removal within 1-foot radius of each bush. No long-term chemical weedicide use.

Pruning & Bush Training

Coppicing at correct intervals to remove diseased/cross branches. Stem training to keep growth upright for easier harvesting. First pruning at 18 months, second at 24 months.

Disease & Pest Monitoring

Regular field walks to detect Rough Bark Disease, Leaf Blight, Pink Stem Borer, and other key threats — with fast targeted treatment before spread occurs.

Ideal for: Landowners and investors who want a hands-off estate that is professionally managed from planting through to first harvest and beyond.

Service 04

GAP Certification & Recognition

A GAP (Good Agricultural Practices) certificate is the gold standard that unlocks premium export markets, higher buyer prices, and government recognition. We guide your entire estate through the documentation, process compliance, and inspection requirements needed to earn it.

Farm Record & Data Management

We implement the record-keeping systems required by GAP — inputs used, application dates, soil test results, harvest logs — to make your estate inspection-ready.

SLS 81:2021 & GI Standard Alignment

Sri Lanka's cinnamon standard requires max 14% moisture, max 5% total ash, min 1.0ml/100g volatile oil. We ensure your cultivation and post-harvest handling meets these benchmarks.

Organic Pathway Consultation

If organic certification is your goal, we structure the entire cultivation from day one using integrated nutrient management — Gliricidia, compost, vermicompost, biochar — to keep your estate chemical-residue free.

Geographical Indication (GI) Positioning

Sri Lanka holds the CCGI 2022 GI certificate for Ceylon Cinnamon. We help your estate documentation align with GI traceability requirements for premium export positioning.

Ideal for: Estate owners targeting export markets, agri-investors requiring traceable supply chains, and farmers wanting to command a premium price for certified Ceylon Cinnamon.

The Journey

From Bare Land to First Harvest

Here is exactly what happens after you contact us — no surprises.

01

Site Visit & Consultation

Week 1

Our team visits your land. We assess terrain slope, soil depth, drainage, existing vegetation, and access. You receive an honest recommendation on the best service package for your specific plot — and a cost estimate.

02

Land Preparation

Weeks 2–6

Clearing, drain installation, soil testing, Dolomite correction, planting position marking, and hole digging. The land is fully ready for plants by the end of this phase.

03

Planting & Establishment

Months 2–3

We supply and plant nursery-grade cinnamon plants timed to the Maha or Yala monsoon onset. Basal fertilizer applied per hole. Thinning at 3 months to retain the strongest plants.

04

Ongoing Maintenance

Months 3–30+

Regular site visits for fertilizer applications, weed management, pruning, pest monitoring, and GAP record-keeping. You receive field reports after each visit so you always know the state of your estate.

First Harvest & GAP Certificate

Year 2–3

Your estate reaches commercial harvest maturity. With GAP documentation in place, your product is traceable, compliant with SLS 81:2021, and positioned for premium local or export buyers.
