Educational Initiative

The Cultivation Hub

Empowering Sri Lankan landowners by installing clear theories of sustainable agriculture. We believe knowledge is the foundation of generational ecological wealth.

Core Curriculum

Open-source agricultural engineering modules for local growers.

Module 01

Zero-Impact Site Preparation

Learn why traditional burning destroys vital soil microbiomes. This module covers proper topographical grading to prevent wet-season topsoil runoff, ensuring your cinnamon roots have a stable, nutrient-dense foundation.

Module 02

Water Table Preservation

Excessive chemical fertilizers leach into community water sources. Discover precision-targeted organic nourishment techniques that deliver exact NPK ratios directly to the plant base, saving money and protecting the ecosystem.

Module 03

Maximum Yield Harvesting

The difference between low-grade and premium Ceylon Cinnamon is in the peel. Master the timing and technique of bark harvesting to maximize essential oil retention and command premium prices in the global market.
